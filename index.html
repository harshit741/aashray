Our Mission and Vision

To work as a catalyst in bringing sustainable change with all possible resources, in the lives of people who are often allowed to languish in neglect and social deprivation.

To rise above our differences and come together, to make a difference in the lives of those who need our help the most.

Our Aim

  1. To combat all social odds and evils.
  2. To work for the betterment of underprivileged.
  3. To help others irrespective of caste, creed Or religion.
  4. To bridge the gap between urban and rural India.
  5. To diversify our efforts to bring in more sunshine in the world.
  6. To catalyst the cause of needy, making them independent and productive.
  7. To encourage our volunteers, to discover the humanity within.

Ground Values

  • Seva Parmo Dharma ( service is the highest religion)
  • Secularism.
  • Empathy, Acceptance & Respect for all living beings.
  • Reliance & Compassion for Human Rights.
  • Reconciliation & Social togetherness.
  • Sincerity & Transparency.
  • Radicalization of young energy.
